About

Rajat A. Sirsikar is a Structural Engineer with over 5 years of experience in the analysis and design of steel structures, pre-engineered buildings, cold-formed steel, and reinforced concrete buildings. His research interests include nonlinear mechanics, finite element analysis, buildings and bridges to contribute toward safer and more resilient infrastructure. He enjoys developing computational tools to make structural engineering more efficient and sharing practical insights from both industry and research.

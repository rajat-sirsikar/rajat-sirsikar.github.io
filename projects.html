Research Focus (PhD)

Current PhD research at IIT Roorkee centres on computational modelling of Train–Track–Bridge interaction. The work combines nonlinear dynamics, finite-element analysis and machine-learning approaches to predict railway-bridge responses under moving train loads, contributing toward safer and more resilient infrastructure.
